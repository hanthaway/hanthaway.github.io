Announcing the Latest Imagine Learning Update

Imagine Learning is proud to announce the official 14.4 and Cloud updates to its award-winning language and literacy software for grades K-6. The official press release for this new software update went live on November 30, 2015.

Version 14.4 features 36 percent more content than that of the previous year. Now, students can learn even more foundational skills, experience more academic rigor via new authentic texts, and enjoy new, engaging books and activities.

As part of this significant product update, educators can now take advantage of The Imagine Learning Reading Level Assessment Powered by the Lexile® Framework for Reading, an important tool that helps monitor and promote student growth in reading.
