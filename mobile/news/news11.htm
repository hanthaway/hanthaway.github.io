Utah Business Magazine’s 2015 “Best Companies to Work For”

Imagine Learning is thrilled to announce our inclusion in Utah Business Magazine’s upcoming “Best Companies to Work For” issue (Dec. 2015).

Companies considered for this award were evaluated rigorously on key workplace elements such as benefits/pay, work opportunities, fairness, corporate culture, employee pride, and internal communication.

The honors also went to companies of varying sizes (micro, small, medium, and large). As a medium-sized company of close to 400 employees, Imagine Learning will be honored at an awards ceremony and reception at the Grand America Hotel in downtown Salt Lake City, Utah on December 10, 2015.

Previous to this announcement, Imagine Learning also ranked number 12 out of 59 Utah companies on the Deseret News/Glassdoor “Best Places to Work” list.

We thank our gracious colleagues at Utah Business Magazine, Deseret News, and Glassdoor for these related honors.
