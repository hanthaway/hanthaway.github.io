2. Buckle my Shoe

福利：后附中英文对照

One, two, buckle my shoe.

一，二，系上我的鞋带

Three, four, shut the door.

三，四，关上门

Five, six, pick up sticks.

五，六，捡起木棒

Seven, eight, open the gate

七，八，打开大门

Nine, ten, big fat hen.

九，十，又大又肥的母鸡
